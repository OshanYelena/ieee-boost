    Frequently asked questions, about the IEEE BOOST 2023

    IEEE Boost is an initiative organised annually by the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student Activities Committee (SLSAC) to provide undergraduates following technical disciplines with a platform to learn about the significant impact of being an IEEE member and better mould their lives in terms of leadership and career goals.

    IEEE Boost 2023 is open to all IEEE enthusiasts across the country!

    The event will be held virtually via Zoom on the 15th and 16th (Saturday and Sunday) of July 2023 from 7 pm onwards.

    No, the event is totally free of charge. Join us to witness a unique virtual experience.

    The organising committee has arranged an array of engaging activities to offer delegates at IEEE Boost 2023 an entirely fresh and immersive experience! This years IEEE Boost also promises a set of interesting and worthwhile sessions that will help you pave the way for a fulfilling volunteering journey within IEEE. Join us and grab this golden opportunity.

    Registrations take place through our website. While we welcome all eager individuals, please note that we take only a limited number of delegates from each Student Branch. We strongly encourage all interested participants to register as soon as possible to avoid missing out on this exciting event!

    At IEEE Boost 2023, you can expect more than just professional development sessions. We have curated a range of activities designed to sharpen your soft skills and foster meaningful connections with fellow like-minded individuals. This event presents the perfect chance to embark on your IEEE volunteer journey!

    IEEE Boost is an annual venture by the Member Activities Subcommittee of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student Activities Committee (SLSAC) to promote the perks of being an IEEE member.
